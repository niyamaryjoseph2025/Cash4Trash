User Guide

Everything you need to know about using Cash4Trash effectively.

1

Create an Account

Visit the login page and enter any username. New users are automatically registered and receive 50 starter credits — no email or password needed.

2

Collect Waste

Gather plastic (bottles, bags, packaging) or bio waste (food scraps, garden waste) from your home or neighborhood. Sort them separately for best results.

3

Submit a Pickup

From your dashboard, select the waste type, enter the approximate weight in kg, choose a pickup date, and optionally leave instructions for the volunteer.

4

Volunteer Picks Up

A volunteer from your area will arrive on the scheduled date, verify the waste, and mark the pickup as complete. You'll see credits in your account immediately after.

5

Earn Credits

Credits are awarded based on weight: 10 cr/kg for plastic and 5 cr/kg for bio waste. Accumulated credits can be redeemed for coupons and rewards.

6

Redeem Rewards

Use credits to get grocery discounts, utility vouchers, or donate to environmental causes. Check the Rewards section in your dashboard for available offers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly are pickups verified?

Most pickups are verified within 24–48 hours of the scheduled date, depending on volunteer availability in your area.

What if my weight estimate is off?

Volunteers carry a scale and will record the actual weight. Credits are awarded based on the verified weight, not your estimate.

Can I submit multiple pickups?

Absolutely. Submit as many pickups as you like — each one earns independent credits upon verification.

How do I become a volunteer?

Login using the "Volunteer" tab on the login page. No approval needed — you can start accepting pickups immediately.

Do credits expire?

Credits remain active for 12 months from the date they're earned. You'll receive a reminder before any credits are about to expire.

Is this service available everywhere?

We're currently active in Bengaluru, Chennai, and Thiruvananthapuram. Expanding to more cities soon — check the Contact page to request your city.
